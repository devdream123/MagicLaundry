About Us

Laundry Magic has established for 30 years in Sydney, NSW Australia. The Laundry Magic has since grown to providing a diverse range of “no stress” services to a variety of prominent businesses throughout the Australia.

Fully aware of the ongoing quality requirements of linen and work wear for many of the industrial, entertainment, catering, hotel and tourism industries, Onyx Commercial Laundry continue to expand and develop new markets and new territories aiming to provide a “one stop” service.

Our valued clients have been responsible for this by means of “word of mouth” on our prompt service and more so the quality and very economical pricing of our service.

Laundry Magic will continue to build upon our history and experience in the industry to provide a fast and effective service that will meet and exceed our client’s expectations.

Our Services

It's simple. You need a dry cleaner where you can drop off your dry cleaning and know that everything will be taken care of. We consistently deliver a high standard of dry cleaning services to our customers. Let us take care of all your dry cleaning needs.

We offer a full repair and alteration service. We can help you with a simple button replacement to a re-lining of a jacket. The most common repairs are shortening of trousers, re-hems of skirts and trousers, button and zip replacements, plus all manner of rips and tears.

We provide convenient, cost effective and simple solutions for your personal laundry requirements, solutions that can give you that extra bit of time in the day to spend with friends and family or maybe the chance to reclaim some of the hours lost to a busy work life.
